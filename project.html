CalmZone

CalmZone is an augmented reality application that simulates a virtual natural space around users that have limited access to real nature. This is an immersive augmented reality experience that helps people relax and meditate in an urban environment.

Role

Individual Project
Research & Design

Time

4 months

Toolkit

Unity 3D
XCode
AR Foundation

Background

With the rapid development of infrastructure in urban communities, natural spaces have become increasingly scarce. According to research, people who live in such urban communities can experience increased stress in their lives due to a lack of exposure to nature. Moreover if these urban residents wish to increase their exposure to nature, they have no choice but to travel long distances away from the city to reach a natural space. CalmZone aims to target this issue of reduced natural spaces in urban communities by providing an accessible way for urban residents to experience nature i.e. in a virtual form.

Design Question

How might we make natural spaces more accessible to urban residents?

Solution

Created as a mobile AR application, CalmZone allows users to create a virtual natural space around themselves directly through their smartphone.

  • Smartphones are more readily available to users compared to specialized equipment like VR headsets.
  • An AR application prevents cybersickness which can take away from the relaxation experience.
  • AR enables the user to transform their environment instead of completely blocking it out. This allows the user to feel at ease in their real environment instead of entering a new space.

Significance

Over time as urbanization has rapidly increased, the negative impact on people’s well-being has also risen. Due to this, there is an increasing need for more exposure to nature. People want to experience environments that are becoming scarce. CalmZone aims to be such a way to help individuals cope with these fast changes in their immediate surroundings and experience the nature that rapidly ceases to exist.
